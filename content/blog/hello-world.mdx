This is the first post on this blog. Consider it a placeholder while I figure out what I want to write about.

## Why write?

A few reasons:

1. **Forcing function** — Writing crystallizes thinking. If I can't explain something clearly, I don't understand it well enough.

2. **Public accountability** — Putting things out there means I'll actually finish them.

3. **Building in public** — I want to document the projects I'm working on, the problems I hit, and how I solved them.

## What to expect

Probably a mix of:

- Technical deep-dives on projects
- ML/CV/systems engineering topics
- Career stuff (internships, job search, etc.)
- Random thoughts that don't fit anywhere else

No promises on frequency. I'll write when I have something worth saying.

Let's see where this goes.
